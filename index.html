Welcome to Symbol Snap!

Everyone's favourite symbol guessing game

Rules

  • You will be presented with a 4x4 grid.
  • Each tile hides a symbol, your goal is to match each symbol with its respective pair.
  • Click on two tiles to open them, if they match they will remain open. If not they will close again.
  • The faster you are the more points you earn, and consecutive matches earn bonus points.
  • Good luck!
  • Important: Wait for the board to refresh after completing a round before quitting to ensure score is saved properly.
